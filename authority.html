What are Authority Scores?

In the HITS (Hyperlink-Induced Topic Search) algorithm, Authority scores identify nodes that are authoritative sources in the network. A node has high authority if:
  • It receives links from many hub nodes
  • It receives links from high-scoring hub nodes
  • It is recognized as an authoritative source of information

Algorithm: HITS | Max Iterations: 500 | Threshold: 1e-4
