# How to Use - Query Examples & Guide

## What to Ask / Query Examples

You don't need to use special syntax. Just type naturally!

### Budget-Based Queries
- "How much will a trip cost for 5000 rupees?"
- "I have 2000 rupees, what can I visit?"
- "Budget is 10000"
- "Affordable destinations under 3000"
- "Expensive trip around 8000"

### Mood/Activity-Based Queries
- "I want to go on an adventure"
- "Looking for a relaxing vacation"
- "Where can I do spiritual meditation?"
- "Cultural and historical sites"
- "Party mood, nightlife"
- "Nature and wildlife experience"
- "Romantic getaway"

### Duration-Based Queries
- "I have 3 days free"
- "4-day trip"
- "Weekend getaway (2 days)"
- "Week-long vacation (7 days)"
- "Just 1 day"

### Location-Based Queries
- "I'm in Mumbai, where should I go?"
- "Starting from Delhi"
- "Within 500km from my location"
- "Near me (within 1000km)"
- "Nearby destinations"

### Combined Queries (Most Useful!)
- "I'm in Mumbai with 5000 rupees, want adventure for 4 days"
- "From Delhi, budget 3000, relaxing mood, 2 days"
- "I'm in Bangalore, 4000 budget, nature experience, 3 days"
- "Near me within 1000km, adventure, 5000 rupees, 4 days"
- "Where I am now, 2000 budget, peaceful, 2 days"

### Special Queries
- "Show me all destinations"
- "Best rated places"
- "Cheapest destinations"
- "Closest places to me"
- "Most popular"

## How the System Works

1. **Enter Your Location** (First Time)
   - You'll be asked "Where are you right now?"
   - Type your city name or area
   - System remembers your location

2. **Enter Your Query**
   - Type what you want naturally
   - Can mention: budget, mood, days, distance, location
   - System extracts constraints automatically

3. **Get Recommendations**
   - Destinations ranked by relevance
   - Distances calculated from YOUR location
   - See budget match, mood match, duration fit
   - View scores for each destination
