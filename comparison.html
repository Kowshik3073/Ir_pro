PageRank vs HITS: Key Insights

Understanding the Differences

PageRank and HITS are different link analysis algorithms that rank nodes based on different principles:
  • PageRank: Measures global importance through recursive link analysis
  • HITS: Separates nodes into authorities and hubs with mutual reinforcement
